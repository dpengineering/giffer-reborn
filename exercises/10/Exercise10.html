Spaghetti and Codeballs

NEW: Spaghetti Code
When you have repetitive sections of code, it can sometimes lead to what's called "spaghetti code." This happens when the logic becomes tangled and hard to follow, much like a bowl of spaghetti.
To avoid this, make sure to keep your code organized and well-commented.
