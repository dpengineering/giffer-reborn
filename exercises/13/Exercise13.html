NEW: Back to Back While Loops
You can have multiple while loops in a row to create more complex patterns. For example, you can have one while loop that lights up LEDs from 2 to 8, and then another while loop that lights them back down from 7 to 3.

Hints

  1. Create a while loop that moves upwards first.
  2. Copy and paste that loop underneath itself. Modify the copy to blink downwards.
  3. Did you flip your inequality symbol?
