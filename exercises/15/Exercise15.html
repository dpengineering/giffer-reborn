Double Blinks

NEW: Blinking Two LEDs at Once
In order to blink two LEDs at the same time, you can use two digitalWrite commands in a row. For example, if you want to blink LED 3 and LED 5 together, you would write: 

        digitalWrite(3, HIGH);
        digitalWrite(5, HIGH);
        delay(500);
        digitalWrite(3, LOW);
        digitalWrite(5, LOW);
        delay(500);
