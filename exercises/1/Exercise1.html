A program that does nothing.

NEW: void setup()
DESCRIPTION:
setup runs only once under these conditions:
  1. When the Arduino is turned on
  2. When the Arduino is reset (find the reset button)
  3. When downloading a program and resetting the Arduino
  4. D13 flashes 3 times to indicate successful reset
NEW: pinMode(2, OUTPUT);
Enables pin 2 on the Arduino to send enough power to turn on an LED. Usage: Arduino may now turn on the LED at pin 2, but this does not actually turn it on. For that, you need: digitalWrite(2, HIGH);
NEW: void loop()
loop() runs over and over forever after setup() finishes.
NEW: delay(5000);
Tells Arduino to wait 5000ms (5 seconds) before going to the next instruction.
