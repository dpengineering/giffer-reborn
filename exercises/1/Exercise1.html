NEW: void setup()
DESCRIPTION:
setup runs only once when the program starts.
Use it to initialize variables, pin modes, etc.
NEW: pinMode(2, OUTPUT);
Enables the LED attached to Arduino's #2 pin to OUTPUT light.
Usage: Arduino may now turn on the LED at pin 2, but this does not actually turn it on. For that, you need: digitalWrite(2, HIGH);
NEW: void loop()
loop() runs over and over forever after setup() finishes.
NEW: delay(5000);
Tells Arduino to wait 5000ms (5 seconds) before going to the next instruction.

Hints

  1. Use digitalWrite(pin, value);
  2. Arduino digitalWrite documentation.
