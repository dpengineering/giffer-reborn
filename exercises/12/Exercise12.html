Backwards Blinking

NEW: Decrementing with x -= 1
When you want to decrease a variable by a certain amount, you can use the -= operator. For example, x -= 1 will decrease the value of x by 1 each time it is executed.
Other examples include x -= 2 (decrease by 2) or x -= 5 (decrease by 5).
