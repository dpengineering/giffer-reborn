Hello to you too LED's 3 and 4

NEW: digitalWrite(pin, value)
digitalWrite(pin, value) sets the specified pin to either HIGH or LOW. HIGH means the pin outputs 5V (or 3.3V on some boards), and LOW means the pin outputs 0V. In this case, a HIGH signal turns our LED on, and a LOW signal turns it off.

Hints

  1. Don't forget to set the appropriate pinMode(pin, value);
